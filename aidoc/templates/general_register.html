Terms and Conditions of Use

This system, named "Artificial Intelligence System for Detecting and Analyzing Oral Cancer," was developed to support the public dental health surveillance project of the Department of Health. The project is overseen by the Intercountry Centre for Oral Health, Department of Health, Ministry of Public Health, Thailand. This system is not a medical device and cannot be used as a substitute for diagnosis by medical doctors or dentists. It remains in the research and development phase, and its results may contain errors or inaccuracies. The development team and associated agencies disclaim all liability for any damages arising from the use of the system. Users are advised to consult medical professionals or dentists if any abnormalities in the oral cavity are observed, and they should not rely on this system's results for medical decision-making. Users under the age of 18 must obtain parental consent before using the system. The system may be updated or improved periodically, which could result in changes to its functionality and outcomes at any time.
