1. This computer program is called Artificial Intelligent System for Detecting and Analyzing PMDs and Oral Cancer. This system is part of a project initiated by the Department of Health to develop an oral public health surveillance system, for screening and evaluating risk factors of precancerous lesions and oral cancer. This project receives a budget and is under the supervision of the Intercountry Centre for Oral Health, Department of Health, Ministry of Public Health

2. (Conditions) The system is not a medical device. The results cannot be used for diagnosis or medical reference in any way. If users find any abnormalities or suspect that there are abnormalities in the oral cavity, they must seek medical advices and recieve proper diagnosis from a licensed doctor or dentist immediately.

3. The system supports only the image data of an oral cavity that are taken with the standard protocol. The size of the lesions must be sufficiently large and clear. It must have appropriate and consistent lighting. With only these conditions, the AI should be able to provide effective prediction results. Users can study how to take an effective oral images in the menu: "Prediction Examples"

4. The system can process only pre-cancerous lesions (Oral Potentially Malignant Disorder or OPMD) and oral cancer lesions, type Oral Squamous Cell Carcinoma (or OSCC) only. Processing of other types of oral lesions is not supported. Example images showing the characteristics of lesions supported by the system will be displayed in the menu: "Prediction Examples"
