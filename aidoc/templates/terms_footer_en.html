You must agree to the following terms and conditions before using our system

1. This computer program is called 'Artificial Intelligent System for Detecting and Analyzing PMDs and Oral Cancer'. The system is part of a project initiated by the Department of Health to develop an oral public health surveillance system, for screening and evaluating risk factors of precancerous lesions and oral cancer for Thai people. This project receives a budget and is under the supervision of the Intercountry Centre for Oral Health, Department of Health, Ministry of Public Health

2. (Conditions) The system is not a medical device. The results cannot be used for diagnosis or medical references in any way. If users find any abnormalities or suspect that there are abnormalities in the oral cavity, they must seek medical advices and recieve proper diagnosis from a licensed doctor or dentist immediately.

3. The system supports only the image data of an oral cavity that are taken with the standard protocol. The size of the lesions must be sufficiently large and clear. It must have appropriate and consistent lighting. With only these conditions, the AI should be able to provide effective prediction results. Users can study how to take an effective oral images in the menu: "Prediction Examples"

4. The AI recognizes only certain lesions including pre-cancerous lesions (Oral Potentially Malignant Disorder or OPMD) and oral cancer lesions, type Oral Squamous Cell Carcinoma (or OSCC) only. Processing of other types of oral lesions is not supported. Example images showing the characteristics of lesions supported by the system will be displayed in the menu: "Prediction Examples"

5. (Conditions) Even though, the system now opens to the public, it is still in the developmental phase. It is currently in the process of researching, collecting more information, and improving the accuracy. It is still considered incomplete and error-prone. The system is presumably expected to give incorrect results any time. The system has not yet been rigorously verified for accuracy by relevant agencies or authorities. Therefore, the International Center for Dental Public Health and the development team will not be responsible for any damages resulting from use in any case.
