To improve the prediction accuracy, please follow the below instructions for taking the oral images

1. Tilt the camera to the horizontal orientation so that the longest side of the image is parallel to the patient's lip.

2. Turn on the camera flash to have sufficient lighting

3. Use high quality cameras so that details inside the oral cavity can clearly be seen and in-focus

4. Submit several and various standard views to the system for the comprehensive results. Follow the examples below

Example of Standard Oral Views

Lip (Front, Inside-Upper, Inside-Lower)

Buccal mucosa (Left and Right)

Gingiva (Left, Front, Right)

Retromolar area (Left and Right)

Hard and Soft palates

Dorsal and lateral tongue (Dorsal, left, right)

Ventral tongue and floor of mouth
